What is Gemini Canvas?

Canvas is a special mode in Gemini that's designed for creating and editing code. Instead of just chatting, Canvas gives you a dedicated workspace where Gemini can generate complete files (like HTML pages) that you can easily copy and use.

How to Enable Canvas:

  2. Log in with your Google account (if you're not already logged in)
  3. Look for the Tools or Extensions icon (usually in the sidebar, looks like a puzzle piece or wrench)
  4. Find "Canvas" in the list of available tools
  5. Toggle it to ON or click "Add"

âœ“ Success Check: You'll know it's working when you see "Canvas" appear as an available option in Gemini's interface. You might see a canvas icon or menu item.
